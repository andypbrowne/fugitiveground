In•for•ma•tion /ˌinfərˈmāSHən/

Fugitive Ground (FG) is a certain time and place everyday beginning one hour before sunset and ending one hour after sunset.

This is a time to collectivize, play games, make art, initiate dialogues and be mindful of the natural cycle from day to night. FG is an attempt to frame everyday experiences while exploring alternate ways to think about time and place.

Lead facilitators will be holding events at different places during FG on different days throughout the year. We encourage you to join us or hold your own event, large or small.
